Mountains...
Every time I’m there I feel complete. Alps are charming, the variety of landscapes and the beauty of nature can’t leave anyone indifferent. Alps spread all over many European countries, and I was so lucky to visit almost all of them.

I’d like to share my experience of these journeys, with hope that some day our life will return back to normal, and I could travel to Alps to feel myself free again.
